What is Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute

Welcome to

Our Training Institute

Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute, Phaphamau, Prayagraj Lucknow Bypass Road was established under Vashitha Educational Foundation. The institute started training program in the year 2024 with an annual intake of 300 students, recognized by Government of India (MSME). Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute is a versatile research institute, offering a wide array of services including consultancy, training, research and analysis, mushroom cultivation, PhD assistance, and more, catering to diverse needs with expertise and dedication.

Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute is an applied and Multidisciplinary Research consultancy provider to transform ideas and theoretical concepts, supported by advanced computational methods and experimental techniques, into translational products. At Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute, we are a team of multidisciplinary, experienced researchers with highly innovative and product-oriented mindsets covering four different research divisions (Science, Humanity, Technology, and Commerce), It includes Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Nanotechnology, Medical chemistry, Therapeutics Vaccines, and Drug formulation, Fundamental Chemistry, Herbal Chemistry, Vedic Mathematics, Sanskrit, Mathematics, Statistics, Cyber Security, Big Data Analysis, Information Technology, Physics, Botany, Zoology, Health Education, Political Science, Sociology, Geography, Social Work, Hindi, Economics, Education, Military Science, Geographic Information Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Commerce. Each division is devoted to providing innovative, customizable, and cost-effective research-oriented products to improve the quality of human life. We incorporate scientific excellence and product development strategy in all that we do. We recognize and understand the importance of providing leading solutions in our research domains. We have a strong track record in delivering scientific insights and reasonable solutions to our clients through the development process. We believe in having a data-driven approach and a collaborative partnership with our clients, working closely to ensure that we provide customized solutions for your unique translational research-oriented product development. Besides, we also offer skill development training on practical and soft skills to assist researchers in their scientific journey.

What we are

Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute is established with a mission to promote research and development as we know India moves towards becoming a knowledge economy, the higher education system is being re-energized to provide hands-on learning experience to students, keeping in view the requirements of the fourth industrial revolution. Our Institute envisions the development of active research communities across disciplines. NEP 2020 also supports the nurturance of research and innovation through hands on training of students, greater industry-academia linkages, and interdisciplinary research. Our believe is that Quanta Multidisciplinary Research and Training Institute meets all the aspects of NEP 2020.
